Manage Customer Service Level ()
Description - Confirm business requirements for all delivered services through documented service descriptions & SLA’s - Align with Service Delivery Management of the customer - Present proposals regarding existing services and feedback performance against existing SLAs. Agree action plan - Develop and enhance relationships with key stakeholders, actively managing their expectations and monitoring their satisfaction levels. - Management of Relationships with key stakeholders as the key interface between Customer and SIAM function - Collect feedback from the business - Provide an uniform approach to Service management and reporting of end to end services
Process ID5
ObjectiveITIL Service Level Management aims to negotiate Service Level Agreements with the customers and to design services in accordance with the agreed service level targets. Service Level Management is also responsible for ensuring that all Operational Level Agreements and Underpinning Contracts are appropriate, and to monitor and report on service levels.
