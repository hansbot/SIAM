Assure Service Continuity ()
Description - Ensuring that the required IT service can be resumed within required and agreed business timescales. - Support the customer’s business continuity planning process and ensure that the recovery arrangements for designated services and components are aligned to identified and agreed business impacts, risks and needs. - Maintain and communicate the IT Service continuity management plan and procedures - Provide support and guidance in fulfilling the IT Service continuity management responsibilities - Review and agree IT Service Continuity Plans with Tower suppliers and service towers
