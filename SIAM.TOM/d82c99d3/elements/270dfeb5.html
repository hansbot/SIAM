Asset & Configuration Management ()
Description - Arrange and manage Service Asset and Configuration Management service review meetings - Work with all parties as requested in audits, impact assessments, investigation and resolution of discrepancies - Liaise with parties and customers to define and agree the CMDB requirements , and ensure any changes to the CMDB structure are processed accordingly - Regularly provide a detailed sample of recent CI updates made to the integrated CMDB - Report high criticality discrepancies to the Service Providers and liaise with the Service Provider to determine actions required to resolve the discrepancy ITIL Service Asset and Configuration Management aims to maintain information about Configuration Items required to deliver an IT service, including their relationships.
