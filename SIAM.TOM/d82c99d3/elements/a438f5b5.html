Coordinate Change & Release Management ()
Description - Manage cross tower and supplier changes and releases - Respond to the customer’s changing business requirements while maximizing value and reducing disruption and re-work. - Respond to the business and IT requests for change / release that will align the services with the business needs. - Coordinate that changes are recorded, evaluated, prioritized and authorized. - Coordinate release calendar is in place and up to date - Coordinate that all changes and releases are recorded and follow-up up in a configuration management system - Manage and control the quality review process for changes
