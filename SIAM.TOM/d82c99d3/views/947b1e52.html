Delivery Org Chart ()
The organizational model will determine the position of SIAM within the organization, and it’s responsibility towards customers and suppliers The SIAM function will need to be a separate unit, ensuring the independence of the integration role from Ciber’s other services The SIAM unit can be headed by the director of managed services, but does not have to be. The SIAM unit will be a small unit as of the start. Rationale is to grow based on customer needs. The core team are fixed resources in the Unit. All other resources are either from other Ciber units or attracted externally The SIAM Unit consists of 4 areas according to the structure of the process model Each having it’s own capabilities and skills There is in the roles no hierarchic order in roles how they are structured. SIAM Supervision oversees the SIAM operations from a management and coordination point of view SIAM projects hands over to SIAM supervision and SIAM operations SIAM support sets the standards for design, Security & compliancy, Finance and and supporting processes
