SIAM Process Model Overview (Level 0) ()
The process model within the SIAM operating model describes the required processes applicable to the SIAM function. The processes can be either core processes or processes as input for the SIAM function. The process model shows their position and how they should interact. The processes as used in the Process Model have been selected by Ciber mainly from the Axelos framework other sources from the market have been added, based on a basis of “best-fit”. The modelling of process groups is designed to serve multiple customers
