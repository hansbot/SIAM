Service catalogue & portfolio management ()
This component is concerned with the creation and management of the Service catalogue and portfolio management that are used by the SIAM provider, the suppliers, and the business to support delivery of the services. The principles, methods and techniques for design and creation of these catalogues are not elaborated here as they are fully described in the ITIL Service Strategy and Service Design publications.
