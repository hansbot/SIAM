Customer Service Level Management ()
Objective: ITIL Service Level Management aims to negotiate Service Level Agreements with the customers and to design services in accordance with the agreed service level targets. Service Level Management is also responsible for ensuring that all Operational Level Agreements and Underpinning Contracts are appropriate, and to monitor and report on service levels.
Proposed GoalTo confirm service level agreements with client (or business)
Proposed GoalTo manage business end users expectations and satisfaction levels
Proposed GoalTo ensure alignment of the services and service level definitions with the service catalogue, the service portfolio and the contracts with service tower suppliers
Proposed GoalTo manage relationships and expectations
