SIAM TOM
SIAM Operating model designed by the Orchestrate group Contains: - Strategic direction and focus - Cornerstones & objectives - Choices of investing in SIAM - Guiding principles - Conceptual model - Operating model building blocks The model gets gradually extended, e.g. with content from ITIL, to add more detail. Most notes on views are a refelction of the powerpoint content of a type a could not map on ArchiMate concepts - e.g. a proposed agenda for a body. Take care that those are bound to the particular view, and not included in the model. When the view gets deleted, or the note is removed from the view, it is gone. Some other notes are temporary comments, such as suggested improvements.
