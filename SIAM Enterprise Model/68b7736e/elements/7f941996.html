Financial Management ()
This component is concerned with financial management of the SIAM provider, the suppliers, and the services, including understanding and monitoring the costs against budgets, accounting, and charging. Typically the business will establish and own the budget, ideally with support from the SIAM provider. The SIAM provider then has responsibility for working within the budgets to deliver the service. One critical aspect is establishing a robust cost model so that the cost of each service, including the SIAM overhead for the service, are known, measured, and monitored. The SIAM provider must be able to justify the costs of its service against its responsibilities and the quality of service delivered.
Proposed GoalEnsures validation of supplier invoicing against delivered services on behalf of the client
Proposed GoalEnsures delivery of financial information necessary for decision making
